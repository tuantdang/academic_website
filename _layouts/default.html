Introduction


I’m working on a Rosie robot (Baxter-based robot), a combination of two 7-DOF arms and AGVs as its base. Rosie is equipped with three distributed computers, five cameras and a Velodyne Lidar. The robot is powered by a battery and wirelessly communicates with other computing devices (mobile/PC/laptop) for monitoring and controlling tasks. The project aims to teach this robot to learn its environment and perform service tasks safely.


Interests
  • 3D perception: 3D object recognition and detection
  • Object manipluation: Tracking objects in 3D and control
